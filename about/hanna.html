Barbra S. Hanna, DO, FACOG


Originally from Philadelphia, Dr. Hanna moved to the Midwest to obtain her medical degree from Des Moines University. She then completed her residency training in Obstetrics and Gynecology at the University of Illinois in Chicago.

Dr. Hanna has always had a passion for womenâ€™s healthcare and gynecologic surgery. Soon after joining a traditional OB/GYN practice in Oak Park upon completion of her residency, she realized she should follow her passion and limit her practice to gynecology. She then joined the Urogynecology Department at Christ Hospital where she served as the Assistant to the Director of Urogynecology. Living in the western suburbs, she learned that her services and expertise were needed in her local community. She joined Associates in Gynecology in 2008 and maintains hospital privileges at Central DuPage Hospital.

Dr. Hanna has a special interest in vaginal and minimally invasive surgery, including vaginal hysterectomy, advanced laparoscopy, laparoscopic hysterectomy and operative hysteroscopy. She enjoys treating pelvic organ prolapse, urinary incontinence, uterine fibroids, abnormal uterine bleeding, endometriosis, abnormal pap smears and pelvic pain. Dr. Hanna believes in a patient-centered approach to the care she delivers. She is proud to treat every patient as she would treat a friend or family member.

When Dr. Hanna is not attending to patients, she loves to bike, play tennis and spend time with her husband and three children.
