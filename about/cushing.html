Heather A. Cushing, MD, FACOG


Dr. Cushing was born and raised in California and earned her undergraduate degrees from the University of California, Davis. She moved to Chicago to attend medical school at Loyola University and completed her residency training in Obstetrics and Gynecology at Lutheran General Hospital in Park Ridge, IL. Upon completion of her residency training, Dr. Cushing joined a private group practice in Park Ridge where she practiced both obstetrics and gynecology for over 10 years.

With a long-standing passion for women’s healthcare – and in particular, the gynecologic issues affecting women of all ages – Dr. Cushing chose to focus the scope of her practice on the gynecologic and surgical needs of her patients. Areas of clinical interest include menopausal issues, uterine fibroids, abnormal pap smear evaluation (colposcopy) and treatment, pelvic organ prolapse, urinary incontinence, abnormal uterine bleeding, pelvic pain, endometriosis, and providing surgical treatment with minimally-invasive techniques such as laparoscopy and vaginal surgery. Dr. Cushing is committed to delivering compassionate and comprehensive care in a supportive environment. She seeks to partner with her patients in their healthcare decisions by providing information and education that is up-to-date and relevant to their unique needs and concerns.

Joining Associates in Gynecology in 2008, Dr. Cushing now enjoys the privilege of practicing medicine in the community in which she lives. She maintains medical staff privileges at Central DuPage Hospital.
