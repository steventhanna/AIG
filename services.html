Our Services


The physicians at Associates in Gynecology are board certified and maintain fellowship in the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology (FACOG). They offer up-to-date evaluation and treatment of the following:


  • Annual Preventative Gynecological Exam
  • Abnormal Pap Smears
  • Contraception
  • Endometriosis
  • Heavy Menstrual Periods
  • Menopausal Care
  • Osteoporosis
  • Ovarian Cysts
  • Pelvic Pain
  • Pelvic Support Problems – uterine prolapse, cystocele, rectocele
  • Peri-menopause
  • Post-Menopausal Bleeding
  • Urinary Incontinence
  • Uterine Fibroids
  • Vaginal Discharge
  • Vulvar Lesions
